How To Play

AIM

  • -Speed through the river rapids, dodge the rocks, and catch bubbles to increase your score-
  • -River rapids get faster periodically-
  • -You have three lives and you lose one everytime you crash against a rock-
  • -You can get more lives by catching the red crosses-
  • -The value of the bubbles increase as the speed of the river increases-

CONTROLS

  • -Use the 'left' and 'right' arrow keys on the keyboard to move sideways-
  • -Pause and un-pause the game by pressing the 'space-bar' key on the keyboard-
  • -Mute and unmute the background music of the game by pressing the 'm' key on the keyboard-
